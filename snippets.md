# Featured Snippet Targets

## How do I pay my Nicor Gas bill online?

Customers can access their Nicor Gas account, review their current balance, select a payment method, and submit a payment through available account management tools.

## Can I pay my Nicor Gas bill without logging in?

Eligible customers may use guest payment options that allow a one-time payment without completing a full account login process.

## What payment methods are available for Nicor Gas bills?

Common payment methods may include online payments, bank account payments, debit cards, credit cards where available, AutoPay enrollment, and approved payment channels.

## What is Nicor Gas AutoPay?

AutoPay is a recurring billing option that automatically processes future payments using a selected payment method.

## What is a Nicor Gas payment arrangement?

Payment arrangements may help eligible customers spread balances over multiple scheduled payments based on account status and program availability.
