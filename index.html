How to Pay Your Nicor Gas Bill Online (4 Easy Steps)

Paying your Nicor Gas bill online via (856)900-7834 is the fastest option and takes about 5 minutes. You can pay with a bank account (free) or a debit/credit card. Here is exactly how to do it.

Open the Nicor Gas bill pay portal

Go to the official Nicor Gas online bill pay page or open the mobile app.

Log in or choose pay-as-guest

Sign in to your Nicor Gas account, or select pay as guest to pay online without logging in using your account number and ZIP code.

Enter your payment details

Add your bank account or debit/credit card information and the amount due.

Review and submit

Confirm the total, submit your Nicor Gas payment, and save the confirmation number.

Pay Nicor Gas Bill by Mail, In Person, or Auto Pay

Pay by mail

Mail a check or money order with your Nicor Gas account number written on it, along with your remittance slip. Allow 5–7 business days for your payment to post, so mail early to avoid late fees.

Pay in person

Pay your Nicor Gas bill in cash at an authorized in-person payment location. In-person payments usually post the same day.

Set up Auto Pay (automatic bank draft)

Enroll in Auto Pay to have your Nicor Gas bill automatically deducted from your bank account each month. It's free, helps you avoid late fees, and ensures you never miss a due date.

Nicor Gas Bill Pay Login & Account Access

A Nicor Gas online account lets you pay your bill, view billing history, track gas usage, enroll in paperless billing, and manage Auto Pay. To log in, visit the official Nicor Gas login page and enter your username and password.

Nicor Gas Bill Pay FAQ

How do I pay my Nicor Gas bill?
You can pay your Nicor Gas bill 6 ways: online, through the mobile app, by phone, by mail, in person, or with Auto Pay (automatic bank draft). Online and app payments are fastest at about 5 minutes.
Can I pay my Nicor Gas bill by phone?
Yes. You can pay your Nicor Gas bill by phone using a checking account or debit/credit card. For guided pay-by-phone help, call the bill pay support line.
Can I pay my Nicor Gas bill online without logging in?
Yes. Nicor Gas offers a pay-as-guest option, so you can make a one-time online payment with just your account number and ZIP code — no login required.
Is there a fee to pay my Nicor Gas bill?
Paying directly from a bank account is typically free. Debit and credit card payments may include a small processing fee charged by the payment processor.
What is the Nicor Gas bill pay phone number?
For live bill pay and payment support, call the help line listed on this page. Always confirm your balance and charges against your official Nicor Gas statement.
When is my Nicor Gas bill due?
Your due date is printed on your monthly Nicor Gas statement. Enroll in Auto Pay or paperless billing reminders so you never miss a payment and avoid late fees.
