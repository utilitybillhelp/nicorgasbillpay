Pay Nicor Gas Bill by Mail, In Person, or Auto Pay

Pay by mail

Mail a check or money order with your Nicor Gas account number written on it, along with your remittance slip. Allow 5–7 business days for your payment to post, so mail payments at least 2 weeks before your due date to avoid late fees. Payments by mail are free.

Pay in person

Pay your Nicor Gas bill in cash at an authorized in-person payment location. In-person payments usually post the same day. Check the official Nicor Gas website for authorized payment locations near you.

Set up Auto Pay (automatic bank draft)

Enroll in Auto Pay to have your Nicor Gas bill automatically deducted from your bank account each month. It's free, helps you avoid late fees, and ensures you never miss a due date. You can cancel Auto Pay anytime from your online account.

Nicor Gas Bill Pay FAQ

How do I pay my Nicor Gas bill?

You can pay your Nicor Gas bill 6 ways: online, through the mobile app, by phone, by mail, in person, or with Auto Pay (automatic bank draft). Online and app payments are fastest, typically taking about 5 minutes. Choose the method that works best for your needs.

Can I pay my Nicor Gas bill by phone?

Yes. You can pay your Nicor Gas bill by phone using a checking account or debit/credit card. For guided, step-by-step support, call (856) 900-7834 available 24/7. Phone payments are instant and often offer the same fee structure as online payments.

Can I pay my Nicor Gas bill online without logging in?

Yes. Nicor Gas offers a pay-as-guest option, so you can make a one-time online payment with just your account number and payment method—no account login required. This is great for one-time payments or if you don't have an account set up.

Is there a fee to pay my Nicor Gas bill?

Paying directly from a bank account is typically free. Debit and credit card payments may include a small convenience fee (usually 2–3% of the payment amount). Auto Pay via bank draft is always free. Mail and in-person payments are also free.

What is the Nicor Gas bill pay phone number?

For live bill pay and payment support, call (856) 900-7834 available 24/7. Always verify the current contact information on the official Nicor Gas website, as phone numbers may change.

When is my Nicor Gas bill due?

Your due date is printed on your monthly Nicor Gas statement. Enroll in Auto Pay or paperless billing reminders so you never miss a payment. Most due dates are 20–25 days after the billing period ends, but always check your statement for your specific date.
